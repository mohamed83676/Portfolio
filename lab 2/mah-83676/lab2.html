Introduction To Utility

My Favorite utility on my Mac has to be the screenshot feature. I use this constantly to capture a memorable moment in a video, take a screenshot of some notes I typed, or just taking a screenshot of an interesting part of a webpage.

How To Download The Utility

This utility is already built in to your mac mac with a series of shortcuts. Some of these shortcuts include

  1. Command+shift+3: Screenshot the whole page.

  2. Command+shift+4: Screenshot a portion of the page

  3. Command+shift+5: You would be able to use this shortcut to accomplish the 2 previous command keys with the added feature of having the ability to take a video of your screen.

General Usage

There are a lot of different usages for screenshots. Personally my biggest is sharing something I saw / hard to describe with friends or just screenshooting something I would like to remember for a later usage.

Pros And Cons

Pros
  • Easy to show what your looking at instead describing whats on the screen
  • Save important information so you don't have to write down
  • Easy to use!
Cons
  • Accidentally take pic of private info
